Our Services

Comprehensive AI-assisted dental screening, triage, education, and referral — all in one platform.

Core Services

Everything from screening to referral

🦷

Tooth Pain Assessment

Our structured symptom questionnaire evaluates the nature, location, duration, and severity of tooth pain. Using clinical decision logic, the system identifies possible causes such as dental caries, pulpitis, or cracked tooth syndrome, and assigns an urgency level for care.

FreeNo registration needed~3 minutes
🩸

Gum Disease Screening

Detects early signs of gingivitis and periodontal disease by assessing bleeding patterns, gum swelling, pain on probing, and tooth mobility. Early detection significantly improves treatment outcomes and prevents tooth loss.

Preventive focusEarly detection
🚨

Emergency Dental Screening

Our dedicated emergency detection module screens for life-threatening conditions including dental abscess with systemic spread, Ludwig's angina, severe dental trauma, and uncontrolled haemorrhage. Patients flagged as emergencies are immediately directed to appropriate care.

Critical feature24/7 available
📍

Clinic Referral & Location Services

After triage, patients are directed to the most suitable nearby dental facility based on their location, urgency level, and required specialty. The system shows distance, opening hours, emergency availability, and allows direct appointment booking.

120+ partner clinicsReal-time availability
🪥

Oral Hygiene Advice And Oral Health Advice

Personalised oral hygiene recommendations tailored to each patient's assessed condition. Covers brushing technique, flossing, diet modification, Take self cleansing fruit e.g Water melon, Cucumber, Brush gently and from the gum line, so as to massage the gingiva, for free flow of blood around the gum and management of sensitivity — all evidence-based and appropriate for the Nigerian context.

PersonalisedEvidence-based

Appointment Booking System

Patients can book dental appointments directly with partner clinics through the platform, with automatic email and SMS reminders. Dentists and clinic admins can manage bookings through the professional portal.

Triage Classification

How we classify urgency

✅

Mild

Minor concern. Home care advice. Routine dental check within 2–4 weeks.

🔶

Moderate

Likely infection or decay. Book a dental appointment within 1–2 weeks.

⚠️

Urgent

Probable abscess or trauma. See a dental professional within 24 hours.

🚨

Emergency

Life-threatening. Go to ER or call 112 immediately.
