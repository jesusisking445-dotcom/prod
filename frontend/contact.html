FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is HomoDentHealth free to use?

Yes — the symptom assessment and educational content are completely free. Some clinics may charge consultation fees for in-person visits booked through the platform.

Is this a replacement for seeing a dentist?

No. HomoDentHealth is a preliminary triage and referral tool. All results should be confirmed by a licensed dental professional.

How is my health data protected?

We use HTTPS encryption, password hashing, and role-based access controls. Your data is never shared with third parties without consent.

Can clinics partner with HomoDentHealth?

Yes! Reach out via the "Clinic Partnership" subject in the form above and our team will contact you with onboarding details.

Medical Disclaimer: Our support team cannot provide medical advice over chat, email, or phone. For dental concerns, use the Symptom Assessment tool or visit a licensed dentist.
