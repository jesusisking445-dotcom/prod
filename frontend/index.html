Symptom Guide

Common conditions we can help identify

Our triage engine maps your symptoms to possible dental conditions — always with a professional referral recommendation.

Symptom Pattern Possible Condition Urgency Level Action
Cold sensitivity, pain on biting Dental Caries (Cavity) Moderate Schedule clinic visit
Swollen, bleeding gums Gingivitis / Periodontal Disease Moderate Professional cleaning
Severe throbbing pain, fever, swelling Dental Abscess Urgent See dentist today
Facial swelling, difficulty breathing Spreading Oral Infection Emergency Immediate ER care
Jaw pain, clicking TMJ Disorder Moderate Dental evaluation
Bad breath, gum swelling Periodontal Infection Mild Oral hygiene + check-up
ℹ️
This table is for educational purposes only. This platform does not provide a final diagnosis. All suggestions must be confirmed by a licensed dental professional.
